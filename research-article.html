Frequently Asked Questions


We at Zonduo are within reach round the clock with the best Ph.D. work assistance and solutions that can bring you the coveted grade.

  • How Research paper is written?

    A typical research article consists of an introduction, literature review, methods, results, discussion, and conclusion sections. Some journals may also require an abstract, acknowledgments, and references section.

  • How do I write an effective abstract?

    An abstract should provide a brief summary of the research question, methods, results, and conclusions of the study. It should be concise and should not exceed 250 words.

  • How do I choose an appropriate journal for my research article?

    Choose a journal that specializes in your field of survey and has a good reputation. Check the journal's guidelines for authors and ensure that your article fits within the scope of the journal.

  • What should I consider while writing the introduction of the article?

    The introduction should provide background information on the research topic, state the research question, and explain the significance of the study. It should also provide an overview of the methods used and the structure of the article.

  • How do I present the results of my research?

    Present the results in a clear and concise manner and use tables, graphs, and figures to help illustrate the results. Ensure that the results are relevant to your study question and that they support your conclusions.

  • How do I write the discussion section?

    The discussion section should interpret the results of the study, relate them to the survey question, and provide insights into the implications of the results. It should also highlight the strengths and limitations of the study and suggest future research directions.
